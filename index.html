Who we are

What could be better than the smell of chocolate brownies that blush in the oven, than the light aroma of lemon zest and the tender taste of cinnamon? We at Mama's Bakery know exactly how to bring you back to childhood. We prepare only the best time-tested goodies. We use natural components and put love into each piece of cake.

And if you want, we will create a cake especially for you according to your personal wishes.
